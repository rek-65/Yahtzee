# 🎲 How to Play Yahtzee

Roll dice, choose a category, and build the best score.

## 🎯 Objective

- Score the most points by filling every category on the score sheet.
- Each category can be used only once.
- Choose carefully because every decision affects your final total.

## 🎲 Turn Flow

- Start your turn by pressing **Roll**.
- You may roll up to **three times** on your turn.
- After each roll, tap dice you want to hold.
- Held dice stay in place while the remaining dice roll again.
- After rolling, choose one score category to lock in your points.

## 🧾 Upper Section

- **ONES** through **SIXES** score the total of matching dice.
- Example: three **FIVES** score **15** points in **FIVES**.
- If the upper section total reaches **63** or more, you earn the upper bonus.

## 🏆 Lower Section

- **THREE OF A KIND** scores the total of all dice when at least three dice match.
- **FOUR OF A KIND** scores the total of all dice when at least four dice match.
- **FULL HOUSE** scores when the dice show a three-of-a-kind plus a pair.
- **SMALL STRAIGHT** scores when four dice form a sequence.
- **LARGE STRAIGHT** scores when all five dice form a sequence.
- **YAHTZEE** scores when all five dice match.
- **CHANCE** scores the total of all dice with no pattern requirement.

## ⚠ Strategy Tips

- Use early rolls to build strong categories before they are gone.
- Do not waste a strong roll on a weak category unless necessary.
- Save **CHANCE** for a turn that does not fit a better category.
- Watch the upper section bonus because it can decide the game.

## ✅ End of Game

- The game ends when every category has been filled.
- Final scores are calculated automatically.
- The player with the highest total wins.
